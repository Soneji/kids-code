What we do:

Our aim is to try and give people the chance to find a book they love and experience the feeling of getting stick in a good book. In a society where mbile phones are now a normality and people spend hours on screens, it is important for people to have a break from their devices and let their imagination go wild. We believe that books are a great way to do this. However, many people are put off the idea of reading a book because it is hard to find the book for you, and not many of us can simply read any old book and enjoy it. So to give those people a chance to read a book we have created a place where you can find the book you want and the book that suits you. Whether you like scifi's or fantasies, whther you are 5 or 50, whether you like novels or short stories, we can find the book for you.
